About JETS!

We are the TAMS Junior Engineering and Technical Society (JETS), TAMS's club for all things STEM. We make an impact in Denton and surrounding areas by helping educate and promote science to young students, with initiatives such as STEM night, SWENext, SciDemos, GEC, STEMBoxes, and our biannual Science Adventure Camps. We also compete in Science Olympiad, including the CyFalls, UT Austin, & Rice Invitationals as well as the Fort-Worth Regionals and State Science Olympiad Tournaments. Additionally, we compete in SciBowl at invitationals and at the regional level. We are excited for the upcoming school year and getting to meet all of you!
